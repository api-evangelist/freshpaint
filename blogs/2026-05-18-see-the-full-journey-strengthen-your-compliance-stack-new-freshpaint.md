Freshpaint released new platform capabilities designed to help healthcare marketers visualize complete patient journeys across channels, strengthen compliance and measurement systems, and expand activation options. Updates include Journeys visualization in Insights, a System Status Dashboard for tracker monitoring, accessibility scanning, compliant URL shortening, privacy-aligned CAPTCHA, custom consent styling, mobile app measurement support, and expanded integrations with partners like Zeta and Nextdoor.
