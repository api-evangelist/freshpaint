Healthcare marketers can run HIPAA-compliant advertising campaigns by understanding privacy regulations and using proper data controls. The key is preventing Protected Health Information from reaching third-party ad platforms through strategies like broad demographic targeting, campaign-level measurement, and compliance technology layers. Organizations that audit their data flows, eliminate leak points, and route information through security gateways can drive growth without regulatory risk.
